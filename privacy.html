Clarke Plumo (hereinafter "Clarke" or "we") values your privacy.

The purpose of this page is to inform users of Squatopia: Full-Body Fitness Roguelike (the "Game") and/or our customer support service (this service and the Game hereinafter referred to as the "Service") regarding our policy with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information to anyone who decided to use our Service.

If you choose to use our Service, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The Personal Information that we collect is used for providing and improving the Service. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use
For a better experience, while using our Game, we may automatically collect anonymized data, that are processed to improve the Game.

Platform Features and Data Processing
When you use Squatopia: Full-Body Fitness Roguelike on Meta Quest, our app requests access to the following Platform Features and processes the associated data:

META QUEST PLATFORM FEATURES:

• User ID: We access your unique Meta user ID to identify you within our game, save your progress, and enable platform features.

• User Profile: We access your Oculus username and profile photo to personalize your in-game experience and display your identity in leaderboards or multiplayer features.

• In-App Purchases and/or Downloadable Content: We process transaction data when you make purchases within the game, including purchase history and payment status.

• Subscriptions: We access subscription-related data including billing period start time, end time, and subscription status to manage your access to premium features.

• Deep Linking: We process data about your desired destination within our app when you access it through external links or invitations.

GAMEPLAY DATA:

• Movement and Motion Data: We process your body movements, position tracking, and motion patterns to enable gameplay mechanics, fitness tracking, and exercise recognition.

• Fitness and Performance Data: We process data related to your workout performance, including squat counts, exercise completion, and workout duration to provide fitness feedback and track your progress.

• Spatial and Environmental Data: We process spatial mapping and boundary data to ensure safe gameplay within your play area.

• Controller Input Data: We process controller position, orientation, and button inputs to enable game interaction and menu navigation.

• Game Progress Data: We process your in-game achievements, level progression, character upgrades, and gameplay statistics to maintain your game state and provide a personalized experience.

All gameplay data is processed locally on your device during gameplay sessions and is essential for the core functionality of our fitness roguelike game. Platform Features data is processed according to Meta's privacy policies and our app's functionality requirements.

By contacting our customer service via e-mail, we will likely receive Personal Information (e-mail address, and any other Personal Information you might have provided us with) that we will not process, except for providing you with the support you requested. The e-mails will be stored to ensure proper follow-up, but you are free to request the Personal Information deletion: clarke.plumo@gmail.com

Data Deletion Rights
You have the right to request deletion of all your personal data that we have collected through our Service. This includes:

• Platform Features data (User ID, Profile, Purchase/Subscription data)
• Gameplay data (Movement, Fitness, Spatial, Controller, and Progress data)
• Customer service correspondence
• Any other personal information collected through the Game

To request deletion of your personal data, please contact us at: clarke.plumo@gmail.com

Upon receiving your request, we will:
1. Verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate
2. Delete all personal data we have collected about you within 30 days
3. Confirm the deletion via email

Please note that some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as transaction records for tax purposes).
